Accomplishments


Accomplishment by our school during our 10th year - 2020  - While we had a few in person dance performances by students and our artistic director during the first few months, with the world quickly moving to virtual mode, NLD adapted to it as well and gave several online performances that were showcased via live streaming by many organizations across the globe. Among other accomplishments within Nritya Laya Darpan, we launched our off shoot "Darpana Reflector Series", a platform for aspiring learners through topic based workshops conducted under esteemed artists like Sri. Vaibhav Arekar, Smt. Vidhya Subramanian, Sri. Parshwanath Upadhye and many other stalwarts. Additionally, Darpana also serves as a platform for young aspirants to showcase their learning and passion. All the sessions were very well received leaving the participants yearning for more. We also took the opportunity to give back to the artiste community by donating a portion of the honorarium to professionals in this field who have been affected by the pandemic. Go to our dedicated page for this platform for details on upcoming workshops & initiatives at DRS.​Launch of our in house publication - The Darpan Gazette bringing together all the students and families with interesting updates, topics on mythology, theory and much more. A candid conversation with our artistic director was also put together as a video interview by the newsletter team.Nritya Sudharnava ~ Nectarine Ocean of Dance an in house performance series was inaugurated under the esteemed presence of Dr. Sridhar Vasudevan. The maiden performance of the series was by our student Kum. Varsha Punati.Between 2021 & 2022, we also staged 4 more students with their Arangetram. Check out the  page  for more details on our student milestones.

Darpan Gazette


 In May 2020, what started as an in house newsletter has now grown into a full fledged Google Site that has been published where the past newsletters have also been chronicled along with a cover page that hosts the  recent events and updates. The Darpan Gazette editorial team consists of the school’s students and they now have 5 editions to their credit and 2 videos - a Candid Talk and a Video montage encapsulating 2020 @NLD. All this is shared and hosted by the team on google site which can be accessed here - The Darpan Gazette. The team has big plans for 2021 like candid talks with experts in this field, games to keep the students engaged and quench their thirst for theoretical knowledge, quizzes to test their mythological understanding, to name a few. All of this has been possible only with your continued support and cheer. For starters, I ask you to please go ahead and visit the site and bookmark it to your favorites and to like, share and subscribe to their channel. Here is their recent video montage of accomplishments at NLD in 2020 - https://youtu.be/BMs-MuLBN_U
